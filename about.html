FAQ's:
Can you play this game alone?

Game Mash is designed for co-op play, where you control two unique characters with distinct abilities. While it's meant to be played with a friend for maximum fun, playing solo is definitely possible. It becomes a true challenge as you manage both characters on your own, balancing their abilities and strategies. Whether you play solo or with a friend, the experience is always exciting!

How many levels are there?

Game Mash features a variety of levels that increase in difficulty as you progress. There are 3 main levels, each with unique environments, enemies, and challenges. As you advance, the levels introduce new mechanics and puzzles, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.

When was this game released?

Game Mash was released in 2025 by a group of students from Saint Xavier University.
