FAQ's:
Welcome to the FAQ section!
Can you play this game alone?

Game Mash is designed for co-op play, where you control two unique characters with distinct abilities. While it's meant to be played with a friend for maximum fun, playing solo is definitely possible.

How many levels are there?

There are 3 main levels with unique environments, enemies, and challenges. Each one becomes more difficult as you progress.

When was this game released?

Released in 2025, Game Mash was developed by a group of students from Saint Xavier University.
